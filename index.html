ORTHOPEDIC PHYSIOTHERAPY

What is Orthopedic Physiotherapy?

Orthopedic physiotherapy focuses on diagnosing and treating injuries, disorders, and dysfunctions related to the musculoskeletal system. It aims to improve mobility, reduce pain, and restore function through manual therapy, exercises, and patient education. Common conditions treated include fractures, sprains, arthritis, and post-surgical rehabilitation. Orthopedic physiotherapists develop personalized treatment plans to help patients regain optimal physical health and function.
